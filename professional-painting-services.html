Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of prep work do you perform before painting?
Our professional painters ensure surfaces are clean, smooth and ready for paint by washing walls, filling holes or cracks, sanding rough spots, and applying primer if necessary.
How do you ensure the safety of both your crew and my home during the painting process?
We follow strict safety protocols which include using proper equipment like ladders and harnesses, laying drop cloths to protect your floors and furniture, and ensuring that all our staff are trained in safe handling of materials and tools.
Can you provide a detailed estimate for the painting job?
Yes, we provide a written estimate outlining the scope of work including surface preparation, materials to be used, number of coats applied, timelines for completion, and the total cost.
Do you use high-quality paints and materials?
Absolutely. We use premium quality paints and materials that ensure durability, longevity, and give a superior finish to your homes surfaces.
What is your policy on satisfaction with the completed job?
Our goal is customer satisfaction. If there are any issues with our work or it does not meet agreed-upon standards, we will address them promptly. We often guarantee our work for a certain period post-completion to ensure peace of mind.
